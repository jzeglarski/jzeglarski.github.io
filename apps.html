Apps

Nexus Tile

Description

Adventure is deep in the jungle. Precious jungle tiles have become incredibly valuable. The right tile mined at the right time can be worth a fortune. If you can mine enough of the right tiles, you can travel deeper into the jungle and discover new tiles. If too many of the wrong tiles build up, game over!

Legend has it, the Nexus Tile can destroy everything in sight. With this stone who knows what you can accomplish.
Good Luck.


~~


Nexus Tile is a puzzle game built around breaking tiles and completing challenges. If you can mine the correct tiles, you'll be able to unlock their powers and reach new heights. Challenge your friends... and your enemies with tiles that you discover. New tiles and levels are constantly emerging so keep coming back to discover them all.
